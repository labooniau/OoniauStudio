## TWUSStock Promotion

TWUSStock 台股美股記帳 is a local bookkeeping app for personal stock portfolio records. It helps users organize transactions, dividends, cost basis, profit and loss, and current holdings.

TWUSStock does not provide trading, order placement, investment advice, asset custody, payment, lending, or cryptocurrency wallet services.

Feature blocks:

- Dual-market records: supports Taiwan and US stock transaction records.
- Local-first data: transaction records are primarily stored on the user's device.
- Import and export: users can export JSON backups and import records from a JSON file.
- Reference quotes: public quote and exchange-rate sources are used for lookup; data may be delayed or incomplete.

## Privacy Policy Summary

TWUSStock does not create user accounts, does not include advertising SDKs, and does not intentionally upload transaction records to Ooniau Studio.

Data handling:

- Local storage: transaction records and settings remain on the device until deleted, app storage is cleared, or the app is uninstalled.
- Network lookup: ticker symbols or search text may be sent to third-party quote services when users use stock quote, stock-name, or exchange-rate lookup.
- Backup files: exported JSON files may contain user-entered financial records. Users are responsible for storing, sharing, and deleting exported files safely.
- Data deletion: users can delete transactions, clear app storage, uninstall the app, and delete exported backup files.

## Full Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-05

TWUSStock is provided by Ooniau Studio as a local stock bookkeeping and portfolio tracking app. This policy explains how the app handles data.

### Data Stored on Your Device

TWUSStock stores stock transaction records and app settings locally on your device. Transaction records may include ticker, market, date, transaction type, price, shares, commission, tax, exchange rate, and notes. App settings may include language preference.

Ooniau Studio does not operate an app server for TWUSStock and does not receive or store your transaction records.

### Network Requests and Third-Party Data Sources

TWUSStock uses internet access to look up stock quotes, stock names, and exchange rates. Depending on the feature you use, the app may send ticker symbols, search text, or quote requests to third-party data sources such as Taiwan Stock Exchange OpenAPI, Taipei Exchange OpenAPI, Yahoo Finance, and Bank of Taiwan exchange rate services.

These requests are used to provide lookup functionality. Third-party services may process technical information, such as IP address, request time, user agent, and requested symbols, according to their own policies.

### Import, Export, and Sharing

TWUSStock lets users export local transaction records as a JSON backup file and import records from a JSON file. Exported files may contain financial information entered into the app. Users are responsible for storing, sharing, and deleting exported backup files safely.

If users choose Android sharing, file picker, cloud storage, or other system-provided features, data may be handled by the selected apps or services.

### Data Sharing

Ooniau Studio does not sell personal or sensitive data. TWUSStock does not include advertising SDKs and does not intentionally share local transaction records with Ooniau Studio.

The app may interact with third-party quote and exchange-rate services when users use lookup features.

### Data Security

TWUSStock stores app data locally on the device and disables Android cloud backup and device-transfer backup for app database, shared preferences, and app files. Network requests use HTTPS endpoints and Android cleartext traffic is disabled.

No method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure. Users should protect their devices and exported backup files.

### Data Retention and Deletion

Transaction records remain on the device until users delete them, clear app storage, uninstall the app, or add/overwrite data through import. Exported backup files remain wherever users save them until deleted by the user.

TWUSStock does not create user accounts, so there is no account deletion flow.

### Children's Privacy

TWUSStock is not designed for children and is not directed to users under 13.

### Changes and Contact

Ooniau Studio may update this policy when app features, data practices, or legal requirements change. For privacy questions, contact Ooniau Studio using the developer contact email shown on the Google Play store listing.

## Financial Disclaimer

Market prices, exchange rates, calculations, and charts may be delayed, unavailable, or inaccurate. Users are responsible for verifying information and making their own investment decisions.
